The UEFA Champions League (disingkat UCL ) adalah sebuah klub tahunan sepak bola kompetisi yang diselenggarakan oleh Uni Asosiasi Sepak Bola Eropa (UEFA) dan diperebutkan oleh klub-klub top Eropa-divisi , memutuskan pemenang kompetisi melalui penyisihan grup round robin untuk memenuhi syarat untuk ganda -format KO berkaki, dan final satu kaki. Ini adalah salah satu turnamen sepak bola paling bergengsi di dunia dan kompetisi klub paling bergengsi di sepak bola Eropa, dimainkan oleh juara liga nasional (dan, untuk beberapa negara, satu atau lebih runner-up) dari asosiasi nasional mereka.

Diperkenalkan pada tahun 1955 sebagai Coupe des Clubs Champions Européens (Prancis untuk Piala Klub Juara Eropa ), dan umumnya dikenal sebagai Piala Eropa , pada awalnya merupakan turnamen sistem gugur langsung yang terbuka hanya untuk juara liga domestik Eropa, dengan pemenangnya diperhitungkan sebagai juara klub Eropa. Kompetisi mengambil nama saat ini pada tahun 1992, menambahkan babak penyisihan grup round-robin pada tahun 1991 dan memungkinkan beberapa peserta dari negara-negara tertentu sejak tahun 1997. [1] Sejak itu telah diperluas, dan sementara sebagian besar liga nasional Eropa masih hanya dapat memasuki kompetisi mereka. juara, liga terkuat sekarang menyediakan hingga empat tim. [2] [3]Klub yang finis di urutan berikutnya di liga nasional mereka, yang tidak lolos ke Liga Champions, memenuhi syarat untuk kompetisi Liga Eropa UEFA tingkat kedua , dan mulai tahun 2021, tim yang tidak memenuhi syarat untuk Liga Eropa UEFA akan lolos ke kompetisi baru. kompetisi tingkat ketiga yang disebut UEFA Europa Conference League . [4]

Dalam formatnya yang sekarang, Liga Champions dimulai pada akhir Juni dengan babak penyisihan, tiga babak kualifikasi dan babak play-off, semuanya dimainkan dalam dua leg. Enam tim yang masih hidup memasuki babak penyisihan grup, bergabung dengan 26 tim yang lolos sebelumnya. Ke-32 tim tersebut diundi ke dalam delapan grup yang terdiri dari empat tim dan bermain satu sama lain dalam sistem round-robin ganda . Delapan juara grup dan delapan runner-up melanjutkan ke fase knockout yang berpuncak pada pertandingan final pada akhir Mei atau awal Juni. [5] Pemenang Liga Champions memenuhi syarat untuk Liga Champions tahun berikutnya, Piala Super UEFA dan Piala Dunia Klub FIFA . [6] [7]Klub Spanyol memiliki jumlah kemenangan tertinggi (18 kemenangan), diikuti oleh Inggris (14 kemenangan) dan Italia (12 kemenangan). Inggris memiliki jumlah tim pemenang terbesar, dengan lima klub telah memenangkan gelar. Kompetisi ini telah dimenangkan oleh 22 klub, 13 di antaranya telah memenangkannya lebih dari sekali dan delapan berhasil mempertahankan gelar mereka. [8] Real Madrid adalah klub paling sukses dalam sejarah turnamen, setelah memenangkannya 13 kali, termasuk lima musim pertamanya dan juga tiga kali berturut-turut dari 2016 hingga 2018. Bayern Munich tetap menjadi satu-satunya klub yang memenangkan semua kompetisi. pertandingan mereka dalam satu turnamen dalam perjalanan menuju kemenangan piala mereka di musim 2019-2020 . [9] Chelsea adalah juara bertahan, setelah dikalahkanManchester City 1-0 di final 2021 .

Edisi pertama Piala Eropa berlangsung selama musim 1955-56 . [18] [19] Enam belas tim berpartisipasi (beberapa dengan undangan): AC Milan (Italia), AGF Aarhus (Denmark), Anderlecht (Belgia), Djurgården (Swedia), Gwardia Warszawa (Polandia), Hibernian (Skotlandia), Partizan ( Yugoslavia ), PSV Eindhoven (Belanda), Rapid Wien (Austria), Real Madrid (Spanyol), Rot-Weiss Essen ( Jerman Barat ), Saarbrücken (Saar ), Servette (Swiss), Sporting CP (Portugal), Stade de Reims (Prancis), dan Vörös Lobogó (Hongaria). [18] [19] Pertandingan Piala Eropa pertama berlangsung pada 4 September 1955, dan berakhir imbang 3–3 antara Sporting CP dan Partizan. [18] [19] Gol pertama dalam sejarah Piala Eropa dicetak oleh João Baptista Martins dari Sporting CP. [18] [19] Final perdana berlangsung di Parc des Princes antara Stade de Reims dan Real Madrid pada 13 Juni 1956. [18] [19] [20]Skuad Spanyol bangkit dari ketinggalan untuk menang 4–3 berkat gol dari Alfredo Di Stéfano dan Marquitos , serta dua gol dari Héctor Rial . [18] [19] [20]

Real Madrid sukses mempertahankan trofi musim depan di stadion kandangnya, Santiago Bernabéu , melawan Fiorentina . [21] [22] Setelah babak pertama tanpa gol, Real Madrid mencetak dua gol dalam enam menit untuk mengalahkan Italia. [20] [21] [22] Pada tahun 1958 , Milan gagal memanfaatkan setelah unggul di scoreline dua kali, hanya untuk Real Madrid untuk menyamakan kedudukan. [23] [24] Final, diadakan di Stadion Heysel , pergi ke perpanjangan waktu di mana Francisco Gento mencetak gol kemenangan pertandingan untuk memungkinkan Real Madrid mempertahankan gelar untuk musim ketiga berturut-turut. [20][23] [24] Dalam pertandingan ulang final pertama, Real Madrid menghadapi Stade Reims di Neckarstadion untuk final 1959 , dan menang 2-0. [20] [25] [26] Klub Jerman Barat Eintracht Frankfurt menjadi tim non-Latin pertama yang mencapai final Piala Eropa. [27] [28] The 1960 akhir memegang rekor untuk gol terbanyak mencetak gol, dengan Real Madrid mengalahkan Eintracht Frankfurt 7-3 di Hampden Park , milik empat gol oleh Ferenc Puskás dan hat-trick oleh Alfredo Di Stefano. [20] [27] [28]Ini adalah gelar kelima berturut-turut bagi Real Madrid, rekor yang masih bertahan hingga saat ini. [8]

Untuk pertama kalinya. [34] [35] [36] Inter Milan mengalahkan Real Madrid tua 3-1 di Ernst-Happel-Stadion untuk memenangkan musim 1963-64 dan meniru keberhasilan lokal-rival mereka. Musim 1969-70 melihat pemenang Belanda pertama dari kompetisi. Klub yang berbasis di Rotterdam , Feyenoord, mengalahkan juara bertahan, Milan di babak kedua, [49] sebelum mengalahkan Celtic di final . [50] Pada musim 1970-71 Ajax memenangkan gelar, mengalahkan tim Yunani Panathinaikos di final. [51] musim melihat sejumlah perubahan, dengan adu penalti diperkenalkan, dan aturan gol tandang diubah sehingga akan digunakan di semua putaran kecuali final. Lagu kebangsaan Liga Champions UEFA, secara resmi berjudul "Liga Champions", ditulis oleh Tony Britten , dan merupakan adaptasi dari lagu kebangsaan George Frideric Handel tahun 1727 Zadok the Priest (salah satu Lagu Kebangsaannya ). [55] [56] UEFA menugaskan Britten pada tahun 1992 untuk mengaransemen lagu kebangsaan, dan lagu tersebut dibawakan oleh Royal Philharmonic Orchestra London dan dinyanyikan oleh Academy of St. Martin in the Fields . [55] Menyatakan "lagu kebangsaan sekarang hampir sama ikoniknya dengan piala", situs resmi UEFA menambahkan bahwa lagu itu "dikenal untuk membuat hati banyak pesepakbola top dunia bergemuruh". [55]

The Champions League anthem is played before the start of each match as the two teams are lined up while the Champions League "starball" logo is displayed in the centre circle.

The chorus contains the three official languages used by UEFA: English, German, and French.[57] The climactic moment is set to the exclamations ‘Die Meister! Die Besten! Les Grandes Équipes! The Champions!’.[58] The anthem's chorus is played before each UEFA Champions League game as the two teams are lined up, as well as at the beginning and end of television broadcasts of the matches. In addition to the anthem, there is also entrance music, which contains parts of the anthem itself, which is played as teams enter the field.[59] The complete anthem is about three minutes long, and has two short verses and the chorus.[57]

Special vocal versions have been performed live at the Champions League Final with lyrics in other languages, changing over to the host nation's language for the chorus. These versions were performed by Andrea Bocelli (Italian) (Rome 2009, Milan 2016 and Cardiff 2017), Juan Diego Flores (Spanish) (Madrid 2010), All Angels (Wembley 2011), Jonas Kaufmann and David Garrett (Munich 2012), and Mariza (Lisbon 2014). In the 2013 final at Stadion Wembley , paduan suara dimainkan dua kali. Di final 2018 dan 2019 , masing-masing diadakan di Kyiv dan Madrid, versi instrumental dari chorus dimainkan, oleh 2Cellos (2018) dan Asturia Girls (2019). [60] [61] Lagu kebangsaan telah dirilis secara komersial dalam versi aslinya di iTunes dan Spotify dengan judul Tema Liga Champions. Pada tahun 2018, komposer Hans Zimmer me-remix lagu tersebut dengan rapper Vince Staples untuk video game EA Sports FIFA 19 , yang juga ditampilkan dalam trailer pengungkapan game tersebut. [62]

Logo "starball" juga dimasukkan ke dalam bola pertandingan resmi kompetisi, Adidas Finale

Pada tahun 1991, UEFA meminta mitra komersialnya, Acara Televisi dan Pemasaran Media (TEAM), untuk membantu "merek" Liga Champions. Ini menghasilkan lagu kebangsaan, "warna rumah" hitam dan putih atau perak dan logo, dan "bola bintang". Bola bintang itu dibuat oleh Design Bridge, sebuah perusahaan yang berbasis di London yang dipilih oleh TEAM setelah sebuah kompetisi. [63]TEAM memberikan perhatian khusus pada detail tentang bagaimana warna dan bola bintang digambarkan di pertandingan. Menurut TEAM, "Terlepas dari apakah Anda seorang penonton di Moskow atau Milan, Anda akan selalu melihat bahan balutan stadion yang sama, upacara pembukaan yang sama yang menampilkan upacara lingkaran tengah 'starball', dan mendengar Lagu Kebangsaan Liga Champions UEFA yang sama". Berdasarkan penelitian yang dilakukan, TEAM menyimpulkan bahwa pada tahun 1999, "logo starball telah mencapai tingkat pengakuan 94 persen di kalangan penggemar". [64]

The UEFA Champions League begins with a double round-robin group stage of 32 teams, which since the 2009–10 season is preceded by two qualification 'streams' for teams that do not receive direct entry to the tournament proper. The two streams are divided between teams qualified by virtue of being league champions, and those qualified by virtue of finishing 2nd–4th in their national championship.

The number of teams that each association enters into the UEFA Champions League is based upon the UEFA coefficients of the member associations. These coefficients are generated by the results of clubs representing each association during the previous five Champions League and UEFA Cup/Europa League seasons. The higher an association's coefficient, the more teams represent the association in the Champions League, and the fewer qualification rounds the association's teams must compete in.

Four of the remaining six qualifying places are granted to the winners of a six-round qualifying tournament between the remaining 43 or 44 national champions, within which those champions from associations with higher coefficients receive byes to later rounds. The other two are granted to the winners of a three-round qualifying tournament between the 11 clubs from the associations ranked 5 through 15, which have qualified based upon finishing second, or third in their respective national league.

In addition to sporting criteria, any club must be licensed by its national association to participate in the Champions League. To obtain a license, the club must meet certain stadium, infrastructure and finance requirements.

In 2005–06, Liverpool and Artmedia Bratislava became the first teams to reach the Champions League group stage after playing in all three qualifying rounds. Real Madrid and Barcelona hold the record for the most appearances in the group stage, having qualified 25 times, followed by Porto and Bayern on 24.[65]

Between 1999 and 2008, no differentiation was made between champions and non-champions in qualification. The 16 top-ranked teams spread across the biggest domestic leagues qualified directly for the tournament group stage. Prior to this, three preliminary knockout qualifying rounds whittled down the remaining teams, with teams starting in different rounds.

An exception to the usual European qualification system happened in 2005, after Liverpool won the Champions League the year before, but did not finish in a Champions League qualification place in the Premier League that season. UEFA gave special dispensation for Liverpool to enter the Champions League, giving England five qualifiers.[66] UEFA subsequently ruled that the defending champions qualify for the competition the following year regardless of their domestic league placing. However, for those leagues with four entrants in the Champions League, this meant that, if the Champions League winner fell outside of its domestic league's top four, it would qualify at the expense of the fourth-placed team in the league. Until 2015–16, no association could have more than four entrants in the Champions League.[67] In May 2012, Tottenham Hotspur finished fourth in the 2011–12 Premier League, two places ahead of Chelsea, but failed to qualify for the 2012–13 Champions League, after Chelsea won the 2012 final.[68] Tottenham were demoted to the 2012–13 UEFA Europa League.[68]

In May 2013,[69] it was decided that, starting from the 2015–16 season (and continuing at least for the three-year cycle until the 2017–18 season), the winners of the previous season's UEFA Europa League would qualify for the UEFA Champions League, entering at least the play-off round, and entering the group stage if the berth reserved for the Champions League title holders was not used. The previous limit of a maximum of four teams per association was increased to five, meaning that a fourth-placed team from one of the top three ranked associations would only have to be moved to the Europa League if both the Champions League and Europa League winners came from that association and both finished outside the top four of their domestic league.[70]

In 2007, Michel Platini, the UEFA president, had proposed taking one place from the three leagues with four entrants and allocating it to that nation's cup winners. This proposal was rejected in a vote at a UEFA Strategy Council meeting.[71] In the same meeting, however, it was agreed that the third-placed team in the top three leagues would receive automatic qualification for the group stage, rather than entry into the third qualifying round, while the fourth-placed team would enter the play-off round for non-champions, guaranteeing an opponent from one of the top 15 leagues in Europe. This was part of Platini's plan to increase the number of teams qualifying directly into the group stage, while simultaneously increasing the number of teams from lower-ranked nations in the group stage.[72]

In 2012, Arsène Wenger referred to qualifying for the Champion's League by finishing in the top four places in the English Premier League as the "4th Place Trophy". The phrase was coined after a pre-match conference when he was questioned about Arsenal's lack of a trophy after exiting the FA Cup. He said "The first trophy is to finish in the top four".[73] At Arsenal's 2012 AGM, Wenger was also quoted as saying: "For me there are five trophies every season: Premier League, Champions League, the third is to qualify for the Champions League..."[74]

Group stage and knockout phaseEdit

 
AC Milan’s Ronaldinho and Zlatan Ibrahimović surrounded by Real Madrid defenders during a Champions League group stage game in 2010

The tournament proper begins with a group stage of 32 teams, divided into eight groups of four.[75] Seeding is used whilst making the draw for this stage, whilst teams from the same nation may not be drawn into groups together. Each team plays six group stage games, meeting the other three teams in its group home and away in a round-robin format.[75] The winning team and the runners-up from each group then progress to the next round. The third-placed team enters the UEFA Europa League.

For the next stage – the last 16 – the winning team from one group plays against the runners-up from another group, and teams from the same association may not be drawn against each other. From the quarter-finals onwards, the draw is entirely random, without association protection. [76]

The group stage is played from September to December, whilst the knock-out stage starts in February. The knock-out ties are played in a two-legged format, with the exception of the final. The final is typically held in the last two weeks of May, or in the early days of June, which has happened in three consecutive odd-numbered years since 2015. In the 2019–20 season, due to the COVID-19 pandemic the tournament was suspended for five months. The format of the remainder of the tournament was temporarily amended as a result, with the quarter-finals and semi-finals being played as single match knockout ties at neutral venues in Lisbon, Portugal in the summer with the final taking place on 23 August.[77]

Changes will be made to the access list above if the Champions League or Europa League title holders qualify for the tournament via their domestic leagues.

  • If the Champions League title holders qualify for the group stage via their domestic league, the champions of association 11 (Turkey in 2019/2020) will enter the group stage, and champions of the highest-ranked associations in earlier rounds will also be promoted accordingly.
  • If the Europa League title holders qualify for the group stage via their domestic league, the third-placed team of association 5 (France) will enter the group stage, and runners-up of the highest-ranked associations in the second qualifying round will also be promoted accordingly.

Piala resmi

Setiap tahun, tim pemenang diberikan Piala Klub Juara Eropa, versi saat ini telah diberikan sejak 1967. Dari musim 1968–69 dan sebelum musim 2008–09 tim mana pun yang memenangkan Liga Champions tiga tahun berturut-turut atau lima kali secara keseluruhan dianugerahi trofi resmi secara permanen. [98] Setiap kali klub mencapai ini trofi resmi baru harus ditempa untuk musim berikutnya. [99] Lima klub memiliki versi trofi resmi : Real Madrid, Ajax, Bayern Munich, Milan dan Liverpool. [98] Sejak 2008, trofi resmi tetap dipegang UEFA dan klub diberikan replika. [98]

The current trophy is 74 cm (29 in) tall and made of silver, weighing 11 kg (24 lb). It was designed by Jürg Stadelmann, a jeweller from Bern, Switzerland, after the original was given to Real Madrid in 1966 in recognition of their six titles to date, and cost 10,000 Swiss francs.

As of the 2012–13 season, 40 gold medals are presented to the Champions League winners, and 40 silver medals to the runners-up.[100]

Prize moneyEdit

As of 2021-22, the fixed amount of prize money paid to the clubs is as follows.[101]

  • Play-off round: €5,000,000
  • Base fee for group stage: €15,640,000
  • Group match victory: €2,800,000
  • Group match draw: €900,000
  • Round of 16: €9,600,000
  • Quarter-finals: €10,600,000
  • Semi-finals: €12,500,000
  • Runner-ups: €15,500,000
  • Champions: €20,000,000

Artinya, klub dapat memperoleh hadiah uang sebesar €85.100.000 di bawah struktur ini, tidak termasuk bagian dari babak kualifikasi, babak play-off, atau kumpulan pasar.

Sebagian besar pendapatan yang didistribusikan dari Liga Champions UEFA terkait dengan "kolam pasar", yang distribusinya ditentukan oleh nilai pasar televisi di setiap negara. Untuk musim 2014–15, Juventus , yang menjadi runner-up, memperoleh total hampir €89,1 juta, di mana €30,9 juta adalah hadiah uang, dibandingkan dengan €61,0 juta yang diperoleh Barcelona, ​​yang memenangkan turnamen dan mendapatkan penghargaan. Hadiah uang €36,4 juta. [102]

SponsorSunting

Sebuah kaleng Heineken dengan branding dari UEFA Champions League Final 2011
Iklan taruhan dilarang di Turki. Pada tanggal 9 April 2013, Real Madrid (yang sponsor kaosnya adalah bwin ) diharuskan mengenakan kaus bebas sponsor saat bermain melawan Galatasaray di Istanbul .

Seperti Piala Dunia FIFA , Liga Champions UEFA disponsori oleh sekelompok perusahaan multinasional, berbeda dengan sponsor utama tunggal yang biasanya ditemukan di liga papan atas nasional. Ketika Liga Champions dibuat pada tahun 1992, diputuskan bahwa maksimal delapan perusahaan harus diizinkan untuk mensponsori acara tersebut, dengan masing-masing perusahaan dialokasikan empat papan iklan di sekeliling lapangan, serta penempatan logo di pra dan wawancara pasca-pertandingan dan sejumlah tiket untuk setiap pertandingan. Ini, dikombinasikan dengan kesepakatan untuk memastikan sponsor turnamen diberikan prioritas pada iklan televisi selama pertandingan, memastikan bahwa setiap sponsor utama turnamen diberikan eksposur maksimum. [103]

From the 2012–13 knockout phase, UEFA used LED advertising hoardings installed in knock-out participant stadiums, including the final stage. From the 2015–16 season onwards, UEFA has used such hoardings from the play-off round until the final.[104]

Adidas is a secondary sponsor and supplies the official match ball, the Adidas Finale, and Macron supplies the referees' kit.[111] Hublot is also a secondary sponsor as the official fourth official board of the competition.[112]

Individual clubs may wear jerseys with advertising. However, only two sponsorships are permitted per jersey in addition to that of the kit manufacturer, at the chest and the left sleeve.[113] Exceptions are made for non-profit organisations, which can feature on the front of the shirt, incorporated with the main sponsor or in place of it; or on the back, either below the squad number or on the collar area.[114]

Jika sebuah klub memainkan pertandingan di negara di mana kategori sponsor yang relevan dibatasi (seperti larangan iklan alkohol di Prancis ), maka mereka harus menghapus logo itu dari kaus mereka. Misalnya, ketika Rangers bermain melawan klub Prancis Auxerre di Liga Champions 1996–97 , mereka mengenakan logo Center Parcs alih-alih Lager McEwan (kedua perusahaan pada saat itu adalah anak perusahaan dari Skotlandia & Newcastle ).
